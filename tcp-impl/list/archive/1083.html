   Date: Thu, 23 Apr 1998 09:33:07 -0700
   From: Craig Partridge <craig@aland.bbn.com>

>I don't know, I just don't feel very comfortable with the proposals
>implicit statement that a flows lower bound should start at 4MSS.

   The draft does *NOT* say this.

   Recall there are three starting window sizes: initial window (IW) used
   in the first slow start, restart window (RW) used in later slow starts done
   after the connection is idle, and the loss window (LW). [This is the
   terminology the group came up with around August 1 of last year]

   The draft proposes setting IW and RW to 4K and leaving LW at 1K.

I understand this, by "flows lower bound" I mean "what is safe to pump
immediately into a starting or restarting connection at once" and
this is what the IW/RW determine.
