    Dear Colleagues:

    For those of you planning to attend INFOCOM in NYC this year, we are holding
    the Wireless Internet Workshop at the Brooklyn campus of Polytechnic
    University on Friday, June 28. The workshop is sponsored by the New York
    State Wireless Internet Center for Advanced Technology (WICAT), which draws
    researchers from Polytechnic and Columbia Universities.

    Registration is free, but please send an email to claudia@catt.poly.edu if
    you intend to come. This will help us in our planning process.

    A tentative program follows.

    Shiv Panwar

    Friday, June 28, 2002

    Free Registration (Please email claudia@catt.poly.edu if you plan to attend)

    Venue: Auditorium Library/CATT Building
    Five Metro Tech Center, Brooklyn, NY

    Tentative Schedule
    9 am Welcome: Phyllis Frankl, Director, Wireless Internet Center for
    Advanced Technology

    9:10 am Keynote Speaker- Joseph Katz, Symbol Technologies

    10 am Invited Speaker 1 - Adam Wolisz, Technical University of Berlin

    10:45 am Coffee Break

    11 am Panel Discussion: Wireless Research after 9/11,
    Chair: Andrew Campbell, WICAT/Columbia University
    Taieb Znati, National Science Foundation
    George Hough, Fire Dept. NY
    Aurel Lazar, Columbia University
    David Johnson, Rice University

    12 pm noon Lunch and Poster Session outside auditorium entrance,
    Tour of Brooklyn Promenade

    1:30 pm Invited Speaker 2 - TBA
    2:15 pm Invited Speaker 3 - David Goodman, Polytechnic University

    3 -3:15 pm Coffee break
    3:15 pm Panel Discussion: Killer Apps for the Wireless Internet
    Chair: Mel Horwich, Polytechnic University
    Panelists: TBA
    4:15 pm: Reception

    For directions and information on WICAT, please visit our website:
    http://wicat.poly.edu
