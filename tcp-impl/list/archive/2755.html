    Dear WSEAS Friends and Members,

    We would like to remind you the following very important meetings of
    WSEAS in the marvelous place of Andalucia Playa, Chiclana, Cadiz,
    Spain, in June 12-16, 2002 (DEADLINE FOR PAPER SUBMISSION:
    MAY 25, 2002, WEB PAGE: http://www.wseas.org )

    * 2002 WSEAS Int.Conf. on SIGNAL PROCESSING, ROBOTICS AND AUTOMATION
    (ISPRA '02)

    * 2002 WSEAS Int.Conf. on MICROWAVES, ANTENNAS AND RADAR SYSTEMS (IMARS
    '02)

    * 2002 WSEAS Int.Conf. on ELECTRONICS AND HARDWARE SYSTEMS (IEHS'02)

    The quality of the conference will be the same as of the other WSEAS Events.
    The Proceedings, Luxurious Post-Conference Books as well as the Special
    issues of WSEAS Trans. will be edited in WSEAS Press and will be of high
    quality and high international circulation.

    We remind very kindly that only in WSEAS Conferences all the accepted papers
    are simultaneously published in two different international fora :
    Proceedings + International Books
     or
    Proceedings + WSEAS Transactions (Journals)

    CONFERENCE PLACE:
    Iberostar Andalucia Playa and Iberostar Royal Andalus: The three joint WSEAS
    International Conferences will be held in a marvelous beach place of
    Andalucia. Here, the Arabic civilization meets the European Spirit creating
    the unique andalucian style in architecture, decoration, painting, music,
    handicraft and in general in every form of fine arts. So, the unique
    historical and exquisite natural surrounding area is the ideal back-drop for
    the conference having Already attracted the interest of academic and
    industrial world. The two linked Hotels (in a walking distance) are new
    hotels in the chain of "Iberostar", on the sea front of Playa de la Barrosa,
    directly on a marvellous sandy beach, next to the Novo Sancti Petri Golf
    Club, the ideal place for golf enthusiasts. The unique architecture of the
    Hotels is inspired by the andalucian traditional architecture providing all
    the modern amenities. The place is ideal for friends of History and Folklore
    Tradition and it is a real paradise for sports-lovers, and for enthusiastic
    fans of nature, of sea and of sun.

    Cadiz: Enjoy the marvellous and historical town with the richest history,
    located in a wonderful place in the strait of Gibraltar and near the famous
    river Guadalkivir. The Phoenicians, the ancient mediterranean seafarers,
    founded Cadix approximately 1,100 years BC. Cadiz holds the distinction of
    being the oldest city in Europe.
    This magnificent town is surrounded by the sea on three sides and is only
    accessible by the "Puerta de Tierra," a strip of land that was constructed
    in 1755 by Torcuato Cayon. Due to the rich history of Cadiz, the monuments,
    buildings and museums are full of stories and depictions of the past. The
    old central quarters is famous for its picturesque charm, and many of the
    buildings reflect the city's overseas links.

    Our Best Regards and Wishes
    See you in Spain this summer
