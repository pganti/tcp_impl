    Dear Madam/Sir,

    It is my pleasure to invite you to submit your recent research work in the fourth International Workshop on Distributed Computing (IWDC 2002) which is being organised in the University of Calcutta, Calcutta, India in December 2002.

    Apart from submission of paper, we would look forward to your co-operation in reviewing the papers. If you kindly agree to review papers, please let us know your area of interest from the topics mentioned in the website of the workshop.

    The Call For Paper and related information are available in www.iwdc2002.org.

    Looking forward to your active participation,

    regards,
