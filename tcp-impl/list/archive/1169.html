I didn't ask my question very well. I am interested in the case where each
of the connections operates at a slow average transmission rate, where we
aren't increasing the aggregate transmit rate, but dividing it over more
connections. The implementation will use UDP, but I figured some of the
scaling issues would be similar to what you would run into if you tried to
push the simultaneous connection limit of a TCP implementation.
