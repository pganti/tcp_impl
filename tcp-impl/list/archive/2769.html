    A MESSAGE FROM THE GENERAL CHAIRMAN, PROF. P.EKEL
    -------------------------------------------------

    The WSEAS (http://www.wseas.org) International Conferences on:

    *** SYSTEM SCIENCE (ICOSYS 2002)

    *** APPLIED MATHEMATICS AND COMPUTER SCIENCE (AMCOS 2002)

    *** POWER ENGINEERING SYSTEMS (ICOPES 2002)

    will be held in Sheraton Hotel, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, October 21-24, 2002.

    Types of Submission Accepted:
    - Papers
    - Special Sessions
    - Tutorials
    - Workshops
    - Panel Discussions

    All submissions are to be done electronically. The submission pages as
    well as other conference details can be found at:

    http://www.wseas.com/conferences/2002/brazil/icosys - ICOSYS 2002

    http://www.wseas.com/conferences/2002/brazil/amcos - AMCOS 2002

    http://www.wseas.com/conferences/2002/brazil/icopes - ICOPES 2002

    All accepted papers are simultaneously published in:
    Conference Proceedings (CD) + Post-Conference Luxurious Edition from the

    Series of International Reference Books and Textbooks
    or
    Conference Proceedings (CD) + WSEAS Transactions (Journals)

    IMPORTANT DEADLINES:
    Submissions due: June 30,2002
    Notification of acceptance: one month after its submission or earlier

    We should be very grateful if you could distribute the information about
    our Conferences among your faculty members and colleagues.
