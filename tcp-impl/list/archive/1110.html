                        ANNOUNCEMENT AND CALL FOR PAPERS

IEEE International Symposium on Wireless Communications for the Next
                                Millennium

                            September 17-18, 1998

                              New Delhi, INDIA

Sponsored by IEEE AES/COM/LEOS India Chapter

In Cooperation with IETE, INDIA, University of Victoria, CANADA, Indian
Institute of Science, BANGALORE.

SCOPE
The scope of this symposium is to provide a timely forum to
comprehensively address the emerging trends in wireless communications and
the potential challenges and possibiliites for the future.
In addition, this conference is intended to bring together practicing
engineers, academics and researchers involved in leading wireless
communication standards and technologies from across Europe, Asia, and
America. India and several other countries in the region would greatly
benefit from such an event, especially when India is looking and planning
to open up for service introduction the emerging and more efficient
wireless technologies for the future. The symposium would also provide a
great business opportunity to international organisations to interact with
Indian government authorities, technical institutes and universities, and
research organisations to more intensely focus on the relevant wireless
technologies and standards.

CALL FOR PAPERS
You are invited to submit technical papers addressing topics of interest
for presentation at the symposium and for publication in the proceedings.
Potential topics include, but not limited to:

. Spectrum and RF Issues
. Wireless Local Loop
. Wireless Multimedia/ATM
. Next Generation Wireless Standards
. Wireless Internet Applications
. Wireless Home and Consumer Applications
. Interoperability of different Wireless Networks
. Mobility and Roaming Issues
. Global Mobile Satellite Sytems and Emerging Trends
. Intelligent NW services in Wireless
. New Services through LMDS
. Performance Analysis of Wireless Networks
. Wireless Access Technologies
. Energy Aware Protocols for Wireless
. Advanced Signal Processing for Wireless
. Intelligent Antennas and Propagation

Please submit 400-500 word summary by June 15, 1998 to one of the following
Chairs. Electronic sumbissions through e-mail is also encouraged.
