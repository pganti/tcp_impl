                              The sixth IFIP
              Communications and Multimedia Security Conference
                                (CMS 2002)

                  Joint working conference IFIP TC6 and TC11

                            September 26-27, 2002
                              Portoroz, Slovenia
         
                        http://www.setcce.org/cms2002/

       ---------------------------------------------------------------
       Call for posters still open! See http://www.setcce.org/cms2002/
       ---------------------------------------------------------------

    CMS 2002 is the sixth IFIP working conference on Communications and
    Multimedia Security since 1995. State-of-the-art issues as well as
    practical experiences and new trends in the areas will be the topics
    of interest again, as proven by preceding conferences. Its two days
    program will feature a single track with 21 full papers. Pre-conference
    tutorial on virtual private networks will also be offered.

    Invited talks given by Dr. Steve Kent (BBN), Prof. Antonio Lioy
    (Politecnico di Torino) and Corinna Schulze (European Commission) will
    provide an insight into the latest results and challenges of
    communications and multimedia security and further stimulate discussion
    among participants from industry and academia.

    The conference will take place in Portoroz, the beautiful Mediterranean
    seaside resort in Slovenia (http://www.portoroz.si). In a walking
    distance from Portoroz, there is the beautiful medieval town of Piran,
    which is one of the most picturesque places in the area.

    See the CMS 2002 conference Web site (http://www.setcce.org/cms2002/)
