In order to publish the larger-initial-window I-D as an Experimental RFC,
the IESG has asked that we also publish draft-ietf-tcpimpl-poduri-01.txt and
draft-shepard-tcp-4-packets-3-buff-02.txt as Informational RFCs, to serve
as supporting documentation. Their authors have revised them to incorporate
the feedback from earlier tcpimpl discussions. Mark & I would now like to
have a Last Call for any final comments, prior to submitting these two to
the RFC editor for publication as Informational.

They're available from:

ftp://ftp.ietf.org/internet-drafts/draft-ietf-tcpimpl-poduri-01.txt
ftp://ftp.ietf.org/internet-drafts/draft-shepard-tcp-4-packets-3-buff-02.txt

- Vern
