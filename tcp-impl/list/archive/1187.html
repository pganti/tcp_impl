> A new one that has since been mentioned is:
>
> sending RST in response to SYN when listen queue full

And let me add another possible one since we just found it because it
upset someones experimental work a bit

        Failing to clear reserved bits. At least one OS turns around
        received frames in some cases. It assumes the reserved bits were
        clear when they may not be. Since an experimental protocol sets
        a bit to say "do you do XYZ" and the reply gets the same bit set
        things break.

        And amusingly of course, all the passive analysis of logfiles never
        showed this bit as being a problem ;)
