Narsimharao Nagampalli wrote:
> We are interested in information regarding Conformance Testing
> Tools and Test Suites for complete TCP/IP stack. Appreciate if
> anybody can throw some pointers.

We're dealing with conformance test of Internet protocols (TCP and
IPv6).
We wrote test cases (~180) for IPv6 in TTCN, which is an abstract
notation developed for conformance test. We also have some test cases
for TCP congestion control (slow start, congestion avoidance, fast
recovery and retransmit) and for connection establishment and closing
are being prepared.

Please, feel free to contact me for further information.
