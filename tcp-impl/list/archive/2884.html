    CALL FOR PAPERS (please, copy, paste and distribute it by email) After the success of the previous relevant conferences of WSEAS on Soft Computing and Artificial Intelligence in Tenerife (Spain), New York (USA), Crete (Greece), Interlaken (switzerland), Las vegas (USA), Cancun (Mexico), WSEAS is proud to announce the: 4th WSEAS International Conference on: NEURAL NETWORKS and APPLICATIONS (NNA '03) 4th WSEAS International Conference on: FUZZY SETS & FUZZY SYSTEMS (FSFS '03) 4th WSEAS International Conference on: EVOLUTIONARY COMPUTATION (EC '03) Lanzarote Island, Canary Islands, Spain.
