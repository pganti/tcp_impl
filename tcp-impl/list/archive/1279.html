Mohit Aron writes:
> > T/TCP is still in experimental status right?
>
> I don't know about other stacks but FreeBSD's network stack already supports
> T/TCP.

It isn't a proposed standard -- the RFCs describing it are of
"experimental" status.

BTW, the distinction between "implements" and "it is a good idea to
use" ought to be made. Just because FreeBSD lets you use it doesn't
mean you should.

Charles Hannum really should submit his "T/TCP Considered Harmful" as
an informational RFC at some point.
