...and N times more aggressive in all subsequent RTTs until the first
packet loss is detected.

> true.. but it does impose a minimum window size..

The proposal is to change the initial window, not the minimum window size
(which can and will be under 4K when recovering from RTOs or when you get
a packet loss and your cwnd is under 8K).
