Wearing my chair hat, I have two comments regarding how the discussion
has proceeded so far:

        1) It is a requirement that those critiquing the I-D should
            have read it. I am uncertain that all of those participating
            in the discussion have done so.

            If not, please do.

        2) The point of the Last Call is *not* to rehash from ground zero
            the extensive discussions we've already had of this change. It
            is to take a last look at the document, the substance of which
            already attained rough consensus, prior to forwarding it to
            the IESG for publication.

            Those participating in the Last Call discussion likewise have
            an obligation to be familiar with the previous discussions,
            as much as possible.

- Vern
