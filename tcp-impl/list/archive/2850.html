    Hi,

    I'm wondering about TCP persist mode.

    As you know, if receiver sends ACK with 0 window size, sender stops
    sending new data.

    Consider this scenario:

    Suppose that sender transmitted segments [1] [2] [3] [4] [5] [6]. Than, receiver sends ack for segment [2] and shrinks window to zero.

    when sender receives it, enters persist mode and stops sending new data.

    After, receiver sends ack for segment [4] and reopens window.

    My questions are:

     

    1) what happens at segment [5] and [6] ? Are they retransmitted by sender? If they are retransmitted, when sender retransmits them? Must the sender wait receiver to reopen window or can retransmits them when it is in persist state?

    2) Could sender shrinks its congestion window, in this scenario?

     

    Thanks in advance.
    P.
