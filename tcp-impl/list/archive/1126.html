At 13:01 -0400 5/8/98, Bachittar Singh Sembi wrote:
>I am interested in tcp performance on win95 based PC for long delay
>links. Therefore, I am looking for a tcpdump implementation that works on
>win95, alternatively what tools exist to monitor tcp on win95 machines.
>

I have used two commercial packages. Etherpeek from the AG Group works
well on Win 95, and the FTP Software On Net package also contains a packet
capture utility. Shawn Ostermann's TCPTRACE program can read traces in the
Etherpeek format.
