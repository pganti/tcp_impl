    Dear colleague:

    We are sending to you the Second International Announcement of the Second International Congress of Telematic to be held in Havana, CUBA next month of November with the central topic "The role of Telematics in Distance Education and in the Computerization of the Society". Several activities of special importance and relevancy are being planned during the Congress.

    We hope this information may be of your personal interest and we would like to thank you if you might forward and advertized it among friends you may consider of interest .

    Willing to clarify any doubt or solicitude of information you may need, receive warm regards from Cuba.

    Sincerely

    Organizing Committee
    Second International Congress of Telematic

    Estimado Colega:

    Adjunto le enviamos el Segundo Anuncio Internacional del II Congreso Internacional de Telemática a desarrollarse en la Habana el próximo mes de noviembre con tema central "El papel de la telemática en la educación a distancia y la informatización de la sociedad" donde se incluyen varias actividades centrales de especial importancia y trascendencia.

    Esperando que esta comunicación sea de su interés personal, a tiempo que agradecemos cualquier reenvío que realice con las personas que considere oportuno.
