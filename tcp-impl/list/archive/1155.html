NASCAR racing is getting so big the action is spilling out of the super speedways and

New tracks have opened in the west, with NASCAR adding five events in new regions. It
has captured a vast audience looking for a sport in which athletes sign autographs for free
and still seem like regular folks.

The explosive growth of NASCAR popularity, coupled with the brand loyalty among fans,
has created an exciting investment opportunity. The Motorsports Associated Growth and
Income Fund seeks to allow investors the ability to take ownership in the sport of
NASCAR.

operations in auto racing. The fund also invests in companies that host or sponsor racing
events. With widespread interest, big and small companies are lapping into this hot
market. It is no longer just a venue for beer, tobacco and tires; 70 of the Fortune 500
companies from Kodak and McDonald's are taking notice to the exciting growth prospects
